About the Book

This book dives deep into a common fear circulating in modern deliverance culture: can a Christian actually be demonized? Rather than simply saying "yes" or "no," this book offers a biblical filter for understanding why, when, and how these experiences happen—without fear, hype, or superstition.

You'll learn how to spot real spiritual deception, understand true spiritual authority, and walk in the freedom that Christ offers through the gospel.

About the Author

Ben Fischer is a pastor and Bible teacher passionate about helping believers walk in confidence, not confusion. He leads Riverview Church in Minnesota and is committed to discipling people in both the Word and the Spirit.
